2026 marks the transition of corporate carbon footprint reporting from optional to business-critical. Key regulatory triggers going live:

- **EU Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism (CBAM)** — goes live in 2026, pricing embedded carbon in imported goods.
- **CSRD (Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive)** — first wave of mandatory disclosures landing for large EU companies.
- **SEC climate disclosure rules** — US shippers representing 60%+ of freight spend now require sustainability data from carriers.
- **New York SB 9072A** — passed NY Senate Feb 10 2026; could make NY the second state with mandatory emissions tracking.

Technology trend: Greenly and similar SME-focused platforms are automating footprint calculation by plugging into common corporate data sources (ERP, travel, utilities). Agentic AI is being adopted for compliance, but introduces audit-integrity concerns (can AI-calculated footprints withstand regulator scrutiny?).

Financial implication: a credible transition plan is now a prerequisite for accessing financing; missing carbon data is a red flag to lenders.
