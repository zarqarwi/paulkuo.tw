Notre Dame University's Institute for Ethics and the Common Good hosted a summit on AI, faith, and human flourishing. Focus on faith-based frameworks for AI ethics, examining how religious traditions can inform responsible AI development.

Topics include: the theological implications of AI consciousness, embodiment, moral agency of machines, and the role of human dignity in AI governance.
