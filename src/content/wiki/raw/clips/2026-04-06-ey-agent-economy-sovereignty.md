EY's 2026 AI trends report covers three intersecting axes: AI sovereignty (nations asserting control over AI infrastructure), agent economy (autonomous AI agents driving business workflows), and regulatory turning points.

Global agentic AI sector expanding from $9.14B (early 2026) to $139B by 2034, CAGR 40.5%. Gartner predicts 40% of enterprise apps will embed AI agents by end of 2026 (up from <5% in 2025). 97% of CEOs report AI initiatives meeting or exceeding expectations.

Telecommunications had highest agentic AI adoption at 48%, followed by retail/CPG at 47%.
