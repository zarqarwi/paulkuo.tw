Google Cloud's AI agent trends 2026 report outlines the state of enterprise AI agent deployment. Aligned with Gartner/IDC data:

- AI agent market crossed $7.6 billion in 2025, projected to exceed $50 billion by 2030.
- 79% of organizations face challenges in adopting AI — a double-digit increase from 2025.
- 54% of C-suite executives admit AI adoption is tearing their company apart.
- Only 29% of organizations see significant ROI from generative AI; just 23% from AI agents.
- Overall AI budget increasing for 86% of respondents in 2026.

Report contextualizes challenges: despite high adoption velocity, companies struggle with integration, governance, and measuring ROI on agentic workflows.
