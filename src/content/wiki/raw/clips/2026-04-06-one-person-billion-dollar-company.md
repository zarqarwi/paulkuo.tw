Matthew Gallagher launched Medvi, a GLP-1 telehealth startup, out of his Los Angeles home in September 2024 with just $20,000, no employees, and more than a dozen AI tools. Medvi posted $401 million in sales in its first full year, amassed 250,000 customers, and produced a 16.2% net profit margin. The company is now tracking toward $1.8 billion in 2026 revenue.

Tools used: ChatGPT, Claude and Grok for code and copy; Midjourney and Runway for ad creative; ElevenLabs for voice-based customer communication, with custom AI agents connecting disparate systems.

At Anthropic's Code with Claude conference, Amodei was asked when the first billion-dollar company with a single human employee would appear, and he replied "2026" with 70-80% confidence.
