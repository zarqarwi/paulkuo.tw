Meta Intelligence overview of Taiwan's AI subsidy landscape in 2026.

Key programs and data:
- "Program for Enhancing SME Smart Business Efficiency" — most direct, lowest-barrier subsidy channel for Taiwan SMEs adopting AI, FY 115 budget NT$310 million
- Focuses on deploying mature, commercially available AI tools (not requiring proprietary R&D)
- All programs draw from "Taiwan AI Action Plan 2.0 (2023–2026)"
- Plan targets: top-3 global position in domain-specific AI chips, NT$250 billion+ in incremental AI hardware/software output
- Qualcomm Innovate in Taiwan Challenge 2026 supports startups in 5G, IoT, ML, smart cities
- Taiwan Tech Arena has incubated 1,000+ startup teams since 2018
- SV Hub Taiwan Demo Day Spring 2026: 1,000+ registrations, 600 in-person, ~200 international investors
