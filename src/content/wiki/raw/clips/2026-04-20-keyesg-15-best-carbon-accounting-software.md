KEY ESG's 2026 survey identifies leading carbon accounting platforms. As climate disclosure requirements tighten and Scope 3 emissions come under greater regulatory scrutiny, carbon accounting software has become a core business system rather than a specialist sustainability tool.

Leading vendors identified:
- Persefoni, Watershed, Normative, Plan A (Plan A Earth), Sweep
- Sinai Technologies, Emitwise, Greenly
- Microsoft Sustainability Manager (Microsoft Cloud for Sustainability)
- IBM Envizi, Avarni, Green Project Technologies
- Terrascope, Unravel Carbon, NetNada

Key 2026 feature set: continuous emissions measurement, supplier engagement, scenario analysis, and disclosure outputs for CSRD, ISSB, TCFD and voluntary frameworks like CDP.

Watershed named leader in Verdantix 2026 Green Quadrant: Enterprise Carbon Management Software report. Greenly emerging as breakout platform for SMEs and mid-market. Microsoft Sustainability Manager bringing carbon accounting into Azure / Power BI / Dynamics stack.
