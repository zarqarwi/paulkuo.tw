"One Person Companies" (OPCs) have seen explosive growth across China in early 2026. By leveraging AI tools for everything from R&D to marketing, these lean startups are injecting fresh vitality into the digital economy.

Chinese cities are offering free apartments and converting idle data centers into incubators as they compete to attract "one-person companies." Government incentives are driving a new wave of solo AI entrepreneurship at scale.
