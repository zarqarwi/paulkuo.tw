Help Net Security's April 2026 analysis of workplace stress trends. Core findings:

- Workplace stress levels in 2026 remain above pre-pandemic baselines
- Multi-year trend of elevated stress despite "return to normal" rhetoric
- Technical/cybersecurity professionals particularly affected (46% burned out in 2026 vs 31% in 2024)
- Connection to AI-era workplace pressure: efficiency gains not translating to reduced workload

Relates to "The Great Stay" phenomenon — workers holding onto current jobs due to fear of job loss, which creates compounding stress by trapping people in environments they want to leave but can't risk exiting.
