The framework, articulated by Jensen Huang and others, in which AI inference tokens become the fundamental economic unit of the intelligence era — replacing human labor hours as the key resource. As token costs drop exponentially, intelligence becomes a commodity, reshaping pricing models, business strategy, and the economics of software production. Token economics underlies the [[one-person-team]] thesis and the disruption of [[software-disruption]].
