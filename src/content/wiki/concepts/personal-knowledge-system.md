The design and practice of building a personal infrastructure for capturing, organizing, and retrieving knowledge — evolving from note-taking apps (Notion, Obsidian) toward AI-native systems that actively synthesize and connect information. The Karpathy Pattern applied to personal knowledge: instead of a static index, an LLM continuously integrates new information into an evolving graph of concepts and entities. This wiki itself is an instance of a personal knowledge system. Related to [[learning-as-meta-skill]], [[content-moat]], and [[tacit-knowledge]].
