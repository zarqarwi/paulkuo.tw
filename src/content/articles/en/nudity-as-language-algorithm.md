In an era dominated by the attention economy.
Nudity is no longer merely erotic, but a "linguistic technology" selected, disseminated, and amplified by algorithms.

This is a cultural dialectic concerning "linguistic sovereignty" and "bodily sovereignty."

## Body as Interface: Self-Preservation Against Neglect

In platform environments where "visible equals existent," nudity becomes a form of self-preservation against algorithmic neglect.
It is not pure desire, but a strategic grammar for seeking survival within the data deluge.

Nudity reduces the body to sensors and data nodes for the attention industry.
This is externally-driven self-deconstruction: seemingly voluntary on the surface, yet systematically reshaped under institutionalized rewards.

## Traffic Insurance and the Algorithm's Skin

For creators, flesh transforms into "traffic insurance" for short-term income.
But this digital policy is built upon the uncertainty of platform rules, liable to expire at any moment.

From McLuhan's perspective, nudity is the algorithm's skin, a data packet that triggers interaction.
The relationship between humans and algorithms has evolved from "user" to "symbiotic organ."

## Dialects and Interpretations of Nude Language

Nudity as language possesses its own grammar and dialects:

**Grammatical Levels**: From implicit nudity to artistic styles, corresponding to different platform reach mechanisms.

**Dialectal Differences**: East Asian "metaphorical grammar" contrasts with Western "intuitive grammar."

Audiences are co-constructors. Their viewing behaviors transform nudity into social codes (such as body positivity or gender liberation). Yet prolonged exposure also leads to aesthetic saturation and sensory cooling.

## Conclusion: A Counter-Grammatical Proposal

If body language has been systematized and standardized.
Humans can only reclaim the right to write the body through "counter-grammatical" actions.

**Creative Strategy**: Challenge recommendation logic through transgressive aesthetics.

**Platform Exodus**: Turn toward decentralized spaces to establish new grammars.

**Educational Pivot**: Incorporate "how platforms shape body language" into media literacy education.

The core of this war is: can we still actively write in this language of nudity?
