## The Life You Envy
### Is Already Someone Else's Miracle

We're always envying others' lives, yet we forget that the days we're living are already miracles in the eyes of others.

#### The Luxury of Civilization's Dividends
Today, your ability to safely browse the internet and enjoy stable electricity and healthcare already far exceeds the daily reality of most people on Earth.
Our frustration over "not being able to afford our dream home" is itself a luxury granted by civilization.

#### The Path to Becoming Unique
Kevin Kelly once said: "Don't try to be the best, try to be the only one."
Rather than being anxious about losing at the starting line, build that path that only you can walk. What's truly valuable is whether you know clearly who you are and how you choose to live.

#### Conclusion
The world isn't fair, but our circumstances are already far better than those of the vast majority of people throughout history.
